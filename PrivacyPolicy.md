# Privacy Policy for Unlink

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

Unlink ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Unlink Android application.

## 1. Accessibility Service API Usage

Unlink uses the Android AccessibilityService API exclusively to detect which application is currently in the foreground to enforce user-configured app blocking schedules. 

**Unlink does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data retrieved from the AccessibilityService.** The Accessibility Service is only active to monitor package names (e.g., `com.instagram.android`) against your locally saved blocking schedules and to provide UI overlays when a distraction is detected. All data processing related to the Accessibility Service happens locally on your device and never leaves your phone.

## 2. Device Administrator Permissions

If you opt-in to "Strict Mode," Unlink may request Device Administrator privileges. This is used solely to prevent the app from being uninstalled during an active focus session. We do not use this permission to wipe data, change passwords, or monitor device activity.

## 3. Data Collection and Storage

Unlink is designed to be privacy-first. 
*   **Local Storage:** All your schedules, block lists, and screen-time statistics are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage.
*   **No Cloud Syncing:** We do not upload your usage habits, focus schedules, or application list to any external servers.
*   **No Third-Party Analytics:** We do not use intrusive third-party trackers to monitor your behavior.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy and providing a notice within the app if the changes are significant.
